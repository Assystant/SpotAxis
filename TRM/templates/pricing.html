What is an ATS?

ATS is the short form of Applicant Tracking System.
ATS is a tool which is used by hiring teams to organize the hiring process.

Will I be charged for "Basic" plan in future?

No, you will never be charged for using our "Basic" plan for lifetime.

How Do I Get Support For "Premium" plan?

The support will be provided via email and the ticket system which is available at the help section. Every email will be replied within 24hrs.

How to pay for premium?

Signup with SpotAxis, choose a plan, have enough credit in your SpotAxis account to cover your current billing and upcoming monthly billings.

SpotAxis understands the power of your brand. The system allows own branding for both Basic and Premium users.
